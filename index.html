Why Annikedel?

Quick and Seamless Onboarding

You can start recording and automating your business finances on the cloud seamlessly through Annikedel and get started quickly either via our API/integrations across tools you are already used to, scan/snap, upload CSV or just manually input your financial data.

Build greater business insights

With Annikedel, you can create reports, financial statements and 1-pager of key financial information and KPIs with just a few clicks. This will help you raise money and file taxes faster so that you can focus on things that matter.

Real-time track of your business metrics and finances

Receive daily remainder to update your books while you can also send custom quotes and invoices to help to get paid faster. Integrate our API for real time metric dashboard, and then track your sales, expenses, user retention. generate financial statements and build your entire data room in one single place.

Annikedel includes

Metric Dashboard

Annikedel allows you to create a single dashboard where you can automatically track your most important KPIs with just a few minutes of effort. You can automatically create, track and manage your business metrics by securely and seamlessly connecting Annikedel with your web platforms or apps, or integrate across tools you use for your business (Google Analytics, Google sheets, etc.). You can also generate a link to share your data room with partners and investors in seconds.

Cash and Expense
Management

By integrating bank account or uploading bank data, you can seamlessly monitor your cash and expense records on the cloud, manage payroll and connect Annikedel with the tools you are already used to.

Invoicing

Whether you are generating, scheduling or tracking multiple invoices, Annikedel makes it seamless to receive money faster, keep financial records and automates your financial model.

Financial Model

Your 3-financial statement model is automatically calculated as you upload bank statement, manage your cash and expense, and upload every needed financial data on the go. You will seamlessly reconcile payables and receivables while your financial reports are automatically updated.
